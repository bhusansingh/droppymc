    OUR GAMES

    LIFESTEAL

    LIFESTEAL If you die, you lose a heart. If you kill someone, you gain a heart. If you get to zero hearts, you are banned from the server until someone revives you, but not everyone is so quick to revive the dead.

    PVP PRACTICE

    PvP practice is a way for players to improve their PvP skills by practicing against other players or bots. Many multiplayer servers offer unranked matches or practice arenas where players can hone their skills
